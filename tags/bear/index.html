Hawaiian Night Bear

The Hawaiian Night Bear (Ursus mauiensis) is a species of bear native to the island of Maui in Hawaii. It is a small bear, with adults weighing between 100 and 150 pounds. Description The Hawaiian Night Bear is a nocturnal creature, emerging from its den at dusk to forage for food. It feeds primarily on fruits, nuts, and insects, and is known for its skill at climbing trees to access hard-to-reach food sources.
